Category: UI/UX Design

Year: 2023

Description: Introduction:
Heritogather is a prototype of a website specially designed to allow users to find out more about their own race and religion, at the same time, to learn more about Singapore's history in order to foster a sense of belonging. Users are also able to to learn about other races and religions recognised in Singapore, to better understand and appreciate the various ethnicity and culture in Singapore.

Key Features:
- Navigation bar with various sections such as Culture, Religion & Cuisines that are furthur split into sub-categories for easier navigation
- Food Recommendations for the various cuisine

Design/Thought Process:
My team and I had the aim of wanting to address the lack of cultural awareness and the shared sense of belonging among individuals in Singapore's society, mainly the inadequate awareness of Singapore's diverse history & culture, as well as the various misconceptions and stereotypes among the different groups. Through this website, we wanted to provide accurate, engaging and yet accessible information about the various cultures, religions and Singapore's history to encourage individuals to better appreciate and understand their own cultural backgrounds.
