Description: Introduction:
This app aims to aid primary school children in their learning, specifically in communication and teaching them how to sign in the alphabet to better communicate with others. The users should be able to navigate through the app easily, address their pain points, entertain the users and simultaneously allow them to learn through the app. For the app to achieve its purpose, utilising of user research, feedback and testing would be essential to ensure that the app is curated for the right target audience, which is the primary school children. The app also needs to have a user-friendly interface and content tailored to suit the needs of the users. In this case, personalisation, gamification, and interactive elements should be added to enhance the users' engagement. CommuniLink is a prototype of an app that aims to aid primary school children in their learning, specifically in communication and teaching them how to sign in alphabets to better communicate with others.
