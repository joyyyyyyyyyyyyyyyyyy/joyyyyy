Category: UI/UX Design

Year: 2023

Description: Introduction:
CommuniLink is a prototype of an app that aims to aid primary school children in their learning, specifically in communication and teaching them how to sign in alphabets to better communicate with others. The user should be able to navigate through the app easily, address their pain points, entertain the users and simultaneously allow them to learn through the app. The app also needs to have a user-friendly interface and content tailored to suit the needs of the users. In this case, personalisation, gamification, and interactive elements are added to enhance the users' engagement.

Key Features:
- Games section with 3 levels of difficulty, where users can play to help them recall what they have learnt in the training section
- Find location feature, helping user to find their way to school/home
- Training section where users can learn how to sign alphabets, numbers and feelings in sign language

Design/Thought Process:
The name, CommuniLink, is a combination of the words "Communicate" and "Link", as the aim of the app is to aid users in their communication, to allow them to communicate and connect better with thier peers. Open Sans Regular is selected for body text due to its excellent legibility and clean appearance, ensuring ease of reading. Open Sans Bold is used to emphasize critical instructions, such as transportation directions, by utilizing its thicker strokes. Proxima Soft Extra Bold is chosen for headers, titles, and high-emphasis elements like game questions, providing a bold, attention-grabbing presence.
