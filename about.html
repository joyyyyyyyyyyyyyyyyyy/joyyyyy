About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Joy, a 3rd year student from Republic Polytechnic, currently pursuing a Diploma in Digital Design & Development.

In school, I am deeply involved in my CCA, SOI Club, where I actively participate in various service learning and workshops held. Additionally, I serve as an EXCO member in the Girls in Tech @ Cross Polytechnic group, focusing on publicity. Outside of academics, I am an avid reader, particularly interested in politics, and I enjoy indulging in Hong Kong dramas. Cybersecurity also holds my fascination. Beyond campus, I serve as an officer with the Girls' Brigade and as a Marketing EXCO with the ISC2 Chapter Youth Wing.
